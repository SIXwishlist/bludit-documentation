Title: Upgrade guide
Position: 3
Content:

1. Make a full backup, all files and directories.
2. Download the latest version from http://www.bludit.com/bludit_latest.zip
3. Extract the zip file.
4. Replace existing files with the new files.
5. Log in to the admin area.
6. Done

<div markdown="1" class="note">
<div class="note-title">IMPORTANT!</div>
After the upgrade, it's highly recommended check your settings.
</div>

<div markdown="1" class="note">
<div class="note-title">NOTE</div>
