Title: How to insert an image
Position: 3
Content:

Create a new post or page, then click the tab **Images** at the right menu.

Click on the button **More images**, drag and drop the image to the gray box or click on it to select an image.

![Drag and drop image](bludit-insert-image-drag-and-drop.png)

Once you have uploaded the image, **double click** on the image to insert it.

![Drag and drop image](bludit-insert-image.png)

<div markdown="1" class="note">
<div class="note-title">NOTE</div>
The code inserted will be different for each HTML Editor.
</div>
