Title: How to schedule a post
Position: 4
Content:

You can prepare a post and schedule it to be published on your web site at a different date.

To schedule a post:
1. Create a new post, **Admin panel->New post**
2. Click **Advanced options** button.
3. Choose the date and time you want the post to be published.
4. Click **Publish** button.
